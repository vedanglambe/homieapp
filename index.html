The problem

Finding a compatible roommate

Most platforms only help with one aspect of moving - and poorly. You either scroll chaotic Facebook groups, cold-message strangers, or browse lifeless listings on Craigslist.

Finding the right apartment

Even when you find a roommate, agreeing on an apartment is another challenge. The process is emotionally exhausting and time-sensitive, with high stakes for both parties.

Finding your space

Navigating NYC's competitive housing market

Making connections

Building meaningful roommate relationships

Finding a compatible roommate in NYC is a time-consuming and often frustrating process. Traditional methods like Craigslist or Facebook groups are inefficient and don't effectively match roommates based on lifestyle preferences, habits, and expectations.

This leads to potential conflicts, dissatisfaction, and even failed living arrangements. Additionally, the process of coordinating viewings, interviews, and paperwork is cumbersome and lacks structure.

The solution: Homies

Homies:Match

A smart, swipe-based roommate discovery system that connects users based on lifestyle compatibility, values, budget, and neighborhoods. Once matched, users can chat, vibe-check, and choose to move forward.

Homies:Hunt

A co-search mode that helps matched roommates find available apartments together, sublets or full leases, powered by real listings and smart filters. No more cold DMs or sketchy listings.

Homies is a comprehensive roommate matching platform that streamlines the process of finding compatible roommates in New York City, making the journey from match to move-in seamless and stress-free.
